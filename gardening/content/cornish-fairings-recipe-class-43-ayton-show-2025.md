**Recipe for Eyemouth Tart (class 43)**

2oz currants 

2oz chopped walnuts

2oz coconut 

1 small teacup sugar

2oz cherries 

1 egg (beaten)

2oz raisins 

1oz margarine (melted)

Line a sandwich tin with shortcrust pastry. Mix fruit, sugar, melted margarine and the beaten egg. Pour mixture into tin. Bake in moderate oven for 30 minutes. Cover with water icing while still hot.
